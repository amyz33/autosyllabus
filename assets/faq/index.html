Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do we create a new syllabus?

Navigate to the front page and click on the "Create Syllabus Now!" button.

2. What if we don't finish filling out the form in one go?

Click on the "Save" button on the form and you can return to the saved form by clicking on the "Load Syllabus Now!" button on the home page.

3. How do we load a previous syllabus?

Go to the syllabus builder and click on the "Load From Previous Save" button.

4. What if the order of the sections is not how I want to format my syllabus?

Remember this builder is just a simple template, once you download the Word Document, you are free to edit any part of it how you like.
